A simple calculator written on C.

** Naming Conventions
*** Constants, Enums, Defines
All caps, snake case.

*** Structs
Title, camel case.

*** Functions, local variables
All script, snake case.

*** Methods
Same as functions. First word is an abbreviation of struct name.
